Arthroben™

Medical Food for the dietary management of osteoarthritis and musculoskeletal inflammation related to athletic or other physical activity.

SKU: MF-ARB330

Description Arthroben™ is a safe, effective, non-NSAID, anti-inflammatory formula offered in a highly absorbable, delicious tasting apple-flavored powder. Its combination of nutrients work to reduce inflammation, stimulate connective tissue repair and increase joint mobility and function.

Benefits of Arthroben™ include:

  • Reduces inflammation
  • Balanced inhibition of COX-1, COX-2, 5-LOX
  • Offers potent antioxidant protection to reduce joint deterioration
  • Increases joint mobility and function
  • Stimulates joint repair, and provides nutritional building blocks for cartilage, ligaments (and skin)
  • Not associated with the negative side effects that are commonly seen with NSAIDs & other drugs
  • Safe for patients on warfarin (Coumadin)
  • Little or no effect on prothrombin times (PT)

Additional Info

Additional Info

Arthroben™ contains flavocoxid, a proprietary blend of natural flavonoids, which provides comprehensive, balanced inhibition of three metabolic pathways (COX-1, COX-2, and 5-LOX), unlike NSAIDs, which cause strong, selective inhibition of only one individual inflammatory pathway. This well-balanced method of action helps to minimize systemic side effects, making it gentle on the GI tract, while effectively managing the metabolic imbalances of osteoarthritis (OA). Flavocoxid also acts as a powerful antioxidant to limit oxidative conversion of arachidonic acid to other damaging metabolites and to quench free radicals in the joints which may break down cartilage and trigger other inflammatory processes in OA.

Arthroben™ also features Fortigel® and Verisol®, two variations of hydrolyzed collagen which boost anabolic processes in connective tissues and inhibits various catabolic enzymes in order help stimulate the repair process. This supports an adequate volume of cartilage as well as the elastic properties of connective tissues, exactly what is necessary to maintain healthy joints.

OsteobenTM

Osteoben™ for the dietary management of osteopenia and osteoporosis

Coming Soon

SKU: MF-OTB120

Description Osteoben™ is formulated to address the pathophysiology of bone loss, providing patients with the proper bone-supportive nutritional requirements in order to safely assist with the dietary management of these conditions. Two key active compounds in Osteoben™ are aglycone genistein and MBP® (milk basic protein), which work to normalize bone turnover and build bone BMD (bone mineral density) in osteopenia and osteoporosis.

Additional Info

Additional Info

Many epidemiological studies have been conducted demonstrating that high isoflavone intake has bone-sparing effects. Research suggests that genistein exerts its effect by managing the metabolic imbalance in bone tissue induced by ovarian hormone depletion, an important fact since the primary type of osteoporosis affects postmenopausal women. Preliminary evidence exists showing aglycone isoflavones such as the genistein in Osteoben™ may be the most potent bone-building components of soy protein, and that increased genistein intake predicted higher BMD, compared to other isoflavones.

MBP® is a multifunctional, natural protein present in trace amounts in human and bovine milk whose main role is to assist the body in its ability to form bone and increase bone density. MBP® helps reinforce bones, making them more receptive to calcium while working to prevent excess calcium from being liberated from the bones. MBP® accomplishes this by helping to increase the number of bone-forming osteoblastic cells while regulating the activity of bone-destroying osteoclastic cells.

Indications and Contraindications

Indications:

Osteoporosis

Osteopenia

Fractures or microfractures of the bone

Contraindications

Pregnant and nursing women

Patients with a history of cancer of the breast or reproductive organs; women who have a history of these cancers in first degree female relatives

MBPs are derived from the whey portion of milk protein, therefore Osteoben™ should be well-tolerated by anyone with allergies/sensitivities to lactose or casein.  The protein components in MBP are unlikely to present any increased risk of allergy for milk allergic subjects or of cross-reactivity for other allergic subjects. Those with actual whey allergies/sensitivities should consult their physician before taking this product.
