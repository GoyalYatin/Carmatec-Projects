Linda Lizotte, RD, CDN

Co-founder and President

Linda Lizotte, cofounder of Designs for Health, is a nationally known nutritionist and speaker. As a nutritionist, she maintained a busy private practice for 15 years, successfully counseling thousands of clients and treating a wide range of health issues including obesity, adrenal burnout, blood sugar problems, and toxic metal overload. Linda has been an expert guest on numerous radio and television programs, and has contributed to several health magazines and nutrition books.

David M. Brady,
ND, DC, CCN, DACBN

Chief Medical Officer

Dr. David M. Brady has 22-years of experience as an integrative clinical practitioner and over 18 years in health sciences academia. He is a practicing naturopathic physician licensed in Connecticut and Vermont and board certified clinical nutritionist. Dr. Brady also received his initial clinical training as a chiropractic physician. He currently serves as the Vice Provost for the Division of Health Sciences, Director of the Human Nutrition Institute, and is an associate professor of clinical sciences at the University of Bridgeport in Connecticut. He maintains a private practice, Whole Body Medicine, in Trumbull, CT. Dr. Brady is also the Chief Medical Officer for Designs for Health, Inc., is an expert consultant to the nutritional supplement and clinical laboratory industries, and is an internationally sought-after presenter on nutritional, naturopathic and integrative medicine. He has appeared on the plenary speaking panel of some of the largest and most prestigious conferences in the field including; IFM, ACAM, A4M, IHS, AANP, NWANP, IAACN, ICC-CIM, ACA-CON, and many more. Dr. Brady has published a multitude of peer-reviewed scientific papers and textbooks related to chronic pain, autoimmunity and functional gastroenterology and is a featured contributing author in the medical textbooks; Advancing Medicine with Food and Nutrients-2nd Ed. (Kohlstadt I, CRC Press, Boca Raton, FL, 2012), Integrative Gastroenterology (Mullin G, Oxford Press, Weil Integrative Medicine Library, New York, NY, 2011) and Laboratory Evaluations for Integrative and Functional Medicine -2nd Ed. (Bralley JA, Lord RS, Metametrix Institute. Duluth, GA. 2008).

Jose A. Llobrera, PhD

Vice President, Research
and Product Education

Dr. Llobrera received his PhD from Texas A&M University and brings with him several years of experience managing research projects. Prior to joining Designs for Health, he spent 5 years in the US nutraceutical industry studying applications of several phytonutrients in cardiovascular health and inflammation.

Robert J. Nicolosi,
Ph.D., CNS

Chief Scientific Officer

Dr. Nicolosi is an Emeritus Professor in the Department of Clinical Laboratory and Nutritional Sciences at the University of Massachusetts Lowell. Dr. Nicolosi has held positions and appointments at prestigious institutions including Harvard School of Public Health, Harvard Medical School, the University of Massachusetts Medical School, Worcester, MA, and the University of Connecticut, Storrs, CT. His major research areas include pharmaceutical and nutraceutical interventions for the treatment and prevention of hypercholesterolemia, cardiovascular disease, age-related macular degeneration, obesity/diabetes and cancer and nanoemulsion technology as it relates to the delivery of pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals and cosmeceuticals. He has approximately 200 publications in these areas.

Andy Casagranda, CN

Director of Product Development

Andy Casagranda is a Certified Nutritionist, having completed his training with American Health Science University (formerly N.I.N.E.) in Aurora, Colorado. Andy has over 17 years of experience in the dietary supplement industry.

Todd LePine, MD

Practitioner Training Consultant

Dr. LePine is a graduate of Dartmouth Medical School and is Board Certified in Internal Medicine, specializing in Integrative Functional Medicine. Additionally, he has advanced clinical training through the Institute for Functional Medicine. Dr. LePine has a private practice located in Lenox, Massachusetts in the Berkshires. The focus in his practice is to help patients achieve optimal health by balancing nutritional biochemistry in the body and to teach people how to adopt healthy lifestyle habits. Dr. LePine is also the clinical medical director for Metametrix Laboratories and teaches seminars to physicians on the clinical application of advanced biochemical laboratory testing.

Alan Weiss, MD

Practitioner Training Consultant

Dr. Alan Weiss serves as the Medical Director of Annapolis Integrative Medicine, and has practiced medicine for over 15 years. Dr. Weiss holds a Bachelor of Arts in biology from the University of Virginia and earned his medical degree from McGill University, completing his internship in integrative medicine at the University of Hawaii and his residency in internal medicine at St. Elizabeth's Hospital of Boston. He is board certified in internal medicine and is a fellow of the American College of Physicians. In the practice of medicine, Dr. Weiss uses an individualized patient-based approach, employing the best of conventional and natural medicine. He has particular interest in working with people dealing with complex or chronic medical issues, including but not limited to fibromyalgia, chronic fatigue syndrome, hormone imbalance, and obesity.


Dr. Weiss is married and has two sons, lives in Annapolis MD, and spends his free time reading, running, practicing yoga and riding his boat on the Chesapeake Bay.


Dr. Weiss is also a senior Program Leader with Landmark Education, having led transformational developmental programs around the world.

Mark Houston,
MD, MS, FACP, FAHA

Medical and Clinical Research Consultant

Dr. Houston is Associate Clinical Professor of Medicine at Vanderbilt University School of Medicine and Director of the Hypertension Institute at Saint Thomas Hospital in Nashville, TN. He graduated from Vanderbilt Medical School, completed his Internal Medicine Training at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF), and served as Chief Resident in Medicine at Vanderbilt University School of Medicine. Dr. Houston is Board Certified in Internal Medicine (ABIM), Hypertension (ASH Specialist in Clinical Hypertension), and Anti-Aging Medicine (ABAAM). He also holds a Masters of Science (MS) degree in Human Nutrition. Having published over 170 medical articles, abstracts, book chapters, and monographs, his recent best-selling books include What Your Doctor May Not Tell You About Hypertension, Handbook of Hypertension for Students and Clinicians, and Vascular Biology for Clinicians. Dr. Houston specializes in hypertension, hyperlipidemia, preventive cardiology, human nutrition, and anti-aging medicine.

Rebecca Murray,
APRN, FNP, MSN, CDE

Practitioner Training Consultant

DAs a Family Nurse Practitioner and Certified Diabetes Educator, Rebecca Murray has over 30 years of experience in the field of diabetes management and the prevention of medical complications associated with diabetes and obesity. She has particular expertise in the area of women's health, specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS), with the goal of achieving optimal hormonal balance and quality of life. Ms. Murray is an Assistant Clinical Professor of Nursing at Yale University School of Nursing (adjunct faculty), and speaks nationally on the subject of metabolic syndrome and the chronic health problems associated with obesity and insulin resistance. She also maintains a private practice focusing on the use of Medical Nutrition Therapy (MNT) for the prevention and treatment of diabetes, cardiovascular disease, and other chronic health problems.

John Peldyak, DMD

Research and Development Consultant

John Peldyak received his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree from Southern Illinois University in 1980. Since then, he has been in private practice dentistry, and also works with the Mobile Dentists in-school program. As a member of the University of Michigan research group (1986-1992), Dr. Peldyak investigated the use of sucrose substitutes for caries prevention. He is also a cofounder of the Academy of Dental Resources, which is known for their innovative dental prevention and continuing education programs. Dr. Peldyak is the author of Sweet Smart (Alternative Sweeteners), Advanced Developments, Inc., 1996.

Cristiana Paul, MS

Product Development Consultant

Cristiana Paul is a nutrition consultant for Designs for Health, serving the Los Angeles area. She holds a Masters in Nutrition Science from Cal Poly Pomona in California, and has 10 years of nutritional counseling experience in clinical practice. Also possessing 10 years of nutrition research experience, Cristiana is relied upon for her contributions to the design and continuous improvement of Designs for Health products.

Camille Gallinger, CN

Product Education Consultant

Camille Gallinger is a Certified Nutritionist with post-graduate training in clinical nutrition from American Health Sciences University and the American Association of Nutrition Consultants (AANC). She is also a Certified Herbalist, having studied at the former Center for Herbal Studies in Corvallis, OR. As a member of the Nutrition Support Team, Camille serves as a writer and nutrition health educator, training Designs for Health customer service representatives. She supports comprehensive recovery by sharing her passion for the principles of nutrition through comprehensive functional/metabolic testing and specific supplement recommendations. Camille also maintains a private nutrition counseling practice, educating both patient groups and medical professionals.

Michael Jurgelewicz,
DC, DACBN, DCBCN

Michael Jurgelewicz, DC, DACBN, DCBCN, has been studying nutrition and wellness for the past 10 years. He has a B.S. degree in Health & Wellness, a B.S. degree in Anatomy, and is a Doctor of Chiropractic. He is Board Certified in Nutrition by the American Clinical Board of Nutrition, and a Diplomate of the Chiropractic Board of Clinical Nutrition. He is also active in continuing education through the Clinical Nutrition Certification Board. Dr. Jurgelewicz’s academic training and clinical experience in functional medicine position him as an authority in the management of a variety of chronic health conditions.
