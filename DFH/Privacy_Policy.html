Privacy Policies

It is the policy of Designs for Health, Inc. to respect your privacy, and the privacy of all visitors to our website.

We firmly believe that maintaining patient and practitioner privacy plays a vital role in exceeding our customers' expectations. For that reason, we have established this policy statement to help you understand our commitment to privacy protection, and the steps we take to ensure it.

Personal Information

It is Designs for Health's policy to collect only:

A. Certain personal information, such as names, addresses, telephone/fax numbers, or e-mail addresses, when that information is voluntarily submitted by our visitors; and

B. Information regarding your use of our website through the use of cookies.

This information will be used only to answer your requests for information, to better understand your needs, to improve our services and to better target our informational offerings. Your personal information will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned or otherwise disclosed to anyone outside Designs for Health and our necessary service providers, except, as follows: Premiere Platform patient direct mail pieces will be distributed only after prior approval by the referring practitioner. A name may be removed from our database simply by contacting Designs for Health at 800-FOR-HEALTH (800-367-4325), or by sending a request for removal to: Customer Service, Designs for Health, Inc., 980 South Street, Suffield, CT 06078. If you elect to provide us with additional information we will collect and use this information for internal purposes only.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies transferred to your computer. These cookies help us to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information, process the items in your shopping cart, understand and save your preferences for future visits, and compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interactions. We may contract with third-party service providers to assist us in better understanding our site visitors. These service providers are not permitted to use the information collected on our behalf except to help us conduct and improve our business.

If you prefer, you can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies via your browser settings. Like most websites, if you turn your cookies off, some of our services may not function properly. However, you can still place orders over the telephone or by contacting customer service.

Aggregated Information

Designs for Health may use the information it collects to generate aggregated information that may be used by Designs for Health to provide insight regarding the usage of our services. For example, we might compile personal information in order to determine the percentage of users living in a particular geographic area. Aggregated information shall be used exclusively for internal purposes.

Children

The Designs for Health site is not intended for, nor is it designed to attract, children under the age of 13. We do not collect personally identifiable data from any person we know to be under the age of 13.

Revisions to this Policy

Designs for Health reserves the right, at our discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this Policy at any time. Any changes in our Policy will be posted here; therefore, we encourage you to visit this section of our site regularly. This Policy was last modified on March 25, 2013.

General

Links from the Designs for Health site may take you to sites not covered by this Privacy Policy. We provide those links as a convenience to you and take no responsibility for the protection of your privacy on said sites. We encourage you to check the Privacy Policy of any site you visit.
