Increase Operational Efficiency

INCREASE OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY

Integrated functionality is the key. The "smart" modules of eHealthPro are designed so that data flows seamlessly, distributing information across the entire platform to simplify every aspect of practice management. Now you can save the time and effort associated with repetitive entries...and you can rest assured that your records are consistently accurate.

eHealthPro streamlines operations to ensure timely, accurate billing and flawless record keeping. With every transaction, eHealthPro gathers relevant data and automatically delivers it to the correct modules for billing, credits, debits, invoices, receipts, inventory, and purchasing. In short, eHealthPro is the ultimate tool for simplification and efficiency, updating all records automatically.

To ensure that participating doctors always have the latest version and highest capability, eHealthPro software can be automatically updated via the Internet.

Centralize Patient Information

Register patients quickly and easily, creating a comprehensive record via manual keyboard entry. Primary features are as follows:

  • Intuitive screens prompt and log all patient data
  • Auto-feeds data to appropriate modules, i.e., patient record, visit, insurance, billing, etc.
  • Meets requirements for accurate SOAP note charting or other record keeping
  • Import digital data from lab reports and tests, including photos, X-rays, ultrasounds, etc.
  • View outstanding balance and credit information through patient record screen

Simplify Scheduling

Intuitive screens and helpful features allow you to easily build and manage appointment schedules. View your practice's entire schedule at a glance, and enjoy the convenience of scheduling appointments months, or even years, in advance.

  • Multiple view and print modes for maximum flexibility
  • Create separate schedules for specific services
  • Access patient's complete visit information, or display full details of any visit with just 1 click
  • Attach comments to individual appointments
  • Automatic e-mail reminder feature helps prevent missed patient appointments and saves time
  • Block out time for meetings, vacations, and other out-of-office periods

Comprehensive Reporting

Harness the power of information. With eHealthPro, it's easy to generate a full range of professional reports that can help you assess and grow your practice:

  • Sales: display sales by day, week, month, quarter, or year
  • Purchasing: display purchases by day, week, month, quarter, or year
  • Inventory: show available stock, preset minimum/maximum levels, cost to your practice, and retail price for all products within eHealthPro
  • Vendors: display all vendors, including contact information
  • Patients: show visits by code, by statement (selectable by Patient or Practitioner), contact information, and birthdays

HIPAA

eHealthPro provides you with documentation and support needed to satisfy particular HIPAA requirements, including HIPAA's Administration Simplification provisions.

The security and privacy of your patients' information is assured through password-controlled access, and built-in security systems block unauthorized access. Access and operation can be easily set and controlled for single or multiple personnel, which helps ensure that any needed changes are completed quickly and accurately.

To learn how eHealthPro can streamline your operations, contact your local Health Educator at 800-FOR-HEALTH ( 800-367-4325 ) to arrange for a FREE demo.
