Premiere Platform

Premium Services in Support of the Integrative Healthcare Practitioner

Register Hereif you are you a Healthcare Practitioner wishing access to our restricted library and professional accounts area detailed below.

Existing DFH Qualified HCPs Login Here

The Designs for Health Premiere Platform is the cornerstone of our Marketing Division, and was developed to enhance and complement the products and services we currently offer our healthcare practitioner (HCP) customers.

Designs for Health considers all of our customers to be a top priority and has developed a support program that benefits all. The Bronze Premiere Platform level allows HCPs to benefit from a unique array of education based resources focused on helping to achieve better patient outcomes and better compliance. As a Silver Premiere Platform account, HCPs can take advantage of an additional group of support services and educational materials to help build their practice and maximize patient compliance. Gold and Platinum Premiere Platform accounts have access to further levels of service that have not only proven to be highly effective, but are quite costly when purchased through business consultants in the industry. The services for which one qualifies are complimentary, brought to you by Designs for Health's dedicated and highly trained staff.

Bronze Level

Access to the private Designs for Health online library, which includes:

  • Practice development tools such as Metabolic Profile testing, practice building discussions, detox, weight loss, and other group programs, private label services, patient diet plans and recipes.
  • Hundreds of recorded hours of Clinical Rounds conference calls with nationally renowned experts in the fields of nutrition and health care.
  • Hundreds of recorded hours of Nutrient Roundtable conference calls exploring clinically relevant topics and the therapeutic use Designs for Health products.
  • Multimedia nutritionist training modules by Dr. David Brady on inflammation, fatigue, detox, gastrointestinal issues, dysglycemia, and cardiovascular health.
  • Product technical data sheets.
  • Condition specific protocols.
  • Patient education materials.
  • Research notes.

Live nutritional practice support

  • Support calls with experienced Designs for Health staff Clinical Nutritionists

Educational Support

  • Access to join weekly Clinical Rounds, Nutrient Roundtable, and Product Education Training live teleconferences

Patient compliance kits

  • Includes patient supplement schedule, food diary, Rx pads, and AutoShip sign up forms
