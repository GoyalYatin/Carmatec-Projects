Nutrition, Nutraceuticals and Neurotransmitters

Download
Dr. Braverman's points of focus in this webinar include:
  • Do you know that you are more susceptible to specific diseases based on neurotransmitters that are low in your body?
  • Dopamine and addictions - time to fix the cause to rid the vicious cycle
  • Resveratrol ("Reverse-it-all") - how this Sirtuin gene activator keeps you young and slim
  • Does depression/anxiety lead to Alzheimer's or does Alzheimer's lead to depression and anxiety?
  • Brain tests to see inside the brain (BEAM)
  • What is your brain speed and how can you increase it to improve your life?
  • Nutrients for raising acetylcholine, dopamine and other neurotransmitters
About Dr. Braverman:
Eric Braverman MD

Eric Braverman, MD known as "America's Brain Doctor" is the director of PATH Medical and PATH Foundation NY, located in New York, NY. He also has a current faculty appointment at Weill Cornell Medical Center in the Department of Neurosurgery. Dr. Braverman is principal investigator with NYU on a study demonstrating the failure of BMI and the superiority of the DEXA scan and the value of leptin blood levels in measuring fitness and obesity. He is also conducting an ongoing study with Weill Cornell Medical College utilizing the PATH Medical brain health checkup to identify cognitive declines after surgery. Dr. Braverman is the author of 130 papers in peer reviewed journals including JAMA, Molecular Psychiatry, Nature, and American Scientist as well as 3 best-selling books: Younger You, The Edge Effect, Younger (Thinner) You.

Dr. Braverman completed medical school at NYU School of Medicine and completed his residency training at Greenwich Hospital Association, Yale Medical School Affiliate. He currently is a fellow of the American College of Nutrition.
