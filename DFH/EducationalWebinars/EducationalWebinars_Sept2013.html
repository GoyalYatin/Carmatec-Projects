New Breakthrough Medical Foods for the Clinical Management of Osteoarthritis and Osteoporosis

Download

Dr. David Brady will provide a comprehensive overview of Designs for Health's first two medical foods products, Arthroben™ and Osteoben™. His presentation will demonstrate how the active ingredients in each of these products provide support for the metabolic processes of osteoarthritis and osteoporosis, respectively - providing patients with safe and effective medical foods for each of these conditions.

In this webinar, you will learn:
  • How to safely modulate the inflammatory process in a balanced manner that provides effective outcomes without the common side-effects commonly encountered with anti-inflammatory medications
  • How to effectively address bone loss and support healthy bone maintenance without the side-effects common with osteoporosis medications
About Dr. Brady:
David Brady ND, DC, CCN, DACBN

Dr. Brady is the Chief Medical Officer for Designs for Health, Inc. He is involved in product formulation, clinical education and support, quality oversight and strategic partnerships for the company. He also serves as Vice Provost of the Division of Health Sciences, the Director of the Human Nutrition Institute, and a Professor of Clinical Sciences at the University of Bridgeport in Connecticut. Dr. Brady has also maintained a private practice specializing in “functional, nutritional and metabolic medicine” for the past 22 years. He has been a featured presenter at virtually all of the premier integrative and nutritional medicine conferences internationally, including IFM, ACAM, A4M, IHS, IAACN, AANP, and many others.

Dr. Brady has been the author and contributor for numerous books and professional publications including Dr. Brady’s Healthy Revolution: What You Really Need to Know to Stay Healthy in a Sick World. He is a contributing author for Integrative Gastroenterology, the first integrative medical textbook on gastroenterology by Johns Hopkins physician Gerard Mullin, MD, and is a contributing author for Laboratory Evaluations for Integrative and Functional Medicine by Lord and Bralley and the second edition of Advancing Medicine with Food and Nutrients, by Ingrid Kohlstadt, MD.

Wednesday, September 11, 2013 – 9pm EDT (6pm PDT)
