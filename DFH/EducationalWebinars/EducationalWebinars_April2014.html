Functional Medicine Approaches to Stealth Infections

Register Now

Date:   Wednesday, April 16th, 

Time:    9pm EST (6pm PST)

Summary:
  • Chronic stealth infections are an emerging health issue in your chronically ill patients, which are often overlooked by both conventional and integrative medicine practitioners. Stealth infections can be connected to a variety of conditions such as autoimmune diseases, chronic fatigue syndrome, arthritis, neurological disorders, cardiovascular disease and many more so called “mystery illnesses.” Infections such as Lyme disease and its co-infections, mycoplasma, chlamydia and viruses are all part of this growing epidemic.
  • Conventional diagnostic methods statistically miss many of these stealth infections leaving the patient without answers. In this webinar Dr. Hedberg will cover some of the best diagnostic methods and clinical indicators of stealth infections. In addition, herbal medicine, nutrition and supplementation strategies for various infections will also be explored.
  • This topic is not only one of the most important aspects of practice today but also the most exciting. Practitioners can quickly expand their practices to a new level by learning and understanding these infection connections.
Bio:
Nikolas R. Hedberg, D.C., D.A.B.C.I.

Nikolas Hedberg, D.C., D.A.B.C.I. is a board certified chiropractic internist by the American Board of Chiropractic Internists. He received his bachelor of science in exercise science from the University of Florida and his Doctor of Chiropractic from Texas Chiropractic College. He is the founder of the Immune Restoration Center in Asheville, NC where he focuses on autoimmune diseases, chronic infections and thyroid disorders. Dr. Hedberg is the author of the book, “TheThyroidAlternative” which is a comprehensive guide to diagnosing and treating autoimmune thyroid disorders.

Dr. Hedberg's passion is the connection between infections and chronic illness which he calls, “The Infection Connection.” Dr. Hedberg is also adjunct faculty at the University of Bridgeport involved in the M.S. in nutrition degree. Dr. Hedberg is the author of the upcoming book Blood Chemistry Analysis for the Integrative Clinician.
