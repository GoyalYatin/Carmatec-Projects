About Ads

In the beginning of the year 2006, ADS opened its doors offering technical and industrial solutions for signage, fabrication, and display, shop fittings, fit out, exhibition, gift and premium. The company brought forward the most recognized and renowned brands to the signage supplies market.

Known for its profound experience over 20 years, ADS represents brands such as Tekcel CNC router from Australia, LaserPro laser engraving and cutting machine from GCC Taiwan, ITC brand CNC tools from U.K, Graphtec brand cutting plotter from Japan, Royal Sovereign Hot and Cold Laminating machine from Korea, Alusign modular sign systems from Belgium, Cleanroom wipes from Berkshire, U.K, Viccam brand CNC engraving and routing machine, Laser and rotary engraving sheet from IPI U.S.A, JRS Frames from U.S.A, Loxeal UV Glue, Redtape double sided tape from Taiwan, Anodized Aluminium sheets from Lorin Industries, U.S.A, Spring digital vinyl, NOVUS Acrylic and Plastic Polish, JINX digital printing ink, Silhouette CAMEOÂ® etc.

Mission

To provide in its area of operation, in a leading way, first class services which are demanded, keeping always the customers delights as its aim.

Vision

Housing advanced technologies designed to give quality and quantity with best after sales support.

ADS continues to pursue the completion of ongoing business activities both in the domestic and overseas markets under various product brands to achieve sustainable and profitable growth. The management expresses their appreciation for the contribution made by the employees to the significant improvement in the operations of the company and from the support received from all other customers, suppliers and business partners. We remain committed to building ADS into a highest level of solution provider in the industry, known for its standard of quality, operational performance and efficiency and admired for its record of consistently creating value for all.

When it comes to after sales support, we deliver peace of mind not empty promises. Whether you need an application question answered or a problem fixed, our team of factory-trained engineers is only ever a phone call away.

With a company that has its hands spread wide over the Middle East, India and Africa, our developments are constantly made known to our customers. We have dealers in Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Iraq, Iran, India etc. for ease of availability for our customers.

We were born in response to meet your demands for simpler, more streamlined ways to run a signage business.
