Powerful Manual or Auto Tool Change Spindles

The Tekcel Verso uses high quality HSD Manual or Auto Tool Change Spindles with ISO 30 tool holders and ER25 or ER32 collets. The spindles are available in either a standard 5kW Manual Tool Change size, the handy 3.8kw Auto Tool Change size or, for a little more, the heavy duty 8kW Auto Tool Change (10hp) motor. HSD Auto Tool Change spindles are capable of vvariable speeds up to 24,000 RPM and feature ceramic bearings for longer life and quieter operation in busy production environments.

Auto Tool Change System - Up to 6 Tools

When choosing the Auto Tool Change option the Tekcel Verso comes with a standard fixed position tool rack that can hold up to six ISO 30 tool holders. This configuration is often more than enough for shopfitters, cabinetmakers and some plastics and sign manufacturers. For those that require more tools, we suggest the Tekcel Enduro model.

Digital Servo / Ball Leadscrew Drive System

Tekcel routers feature recirculating ball leadscrew drive systems powered by closed loop, digital servo control motors on all axes. The X axis has 2 leadscrew drives to power the heavy duty gantry up and down the bed. The ball leadscrew drive systems deliver unerring accuracy, repeatability and reliability. Their rolling ball design virtually eliminates contact wear and creates a robust linear motion free of play. Less expensive drive systems, such as rack and pinion, are subject to wear and, as a result, can end up costing considerably more in terms of loss of accuracy, breakdowns and loss of production. Our servo motors deliver the power you need to get the through the biggest workloads, constantly checking their position to maintain cutting accuracy at all times.

Sheet Material Locating System

The Tekcel Verso incorporates pop-up locating pins as standard. Many other machines only offer this feature as a paid option, but it comes standard on the Verso. Simply pop the pins into the up position, slide the board against the pins at the front and side of the machine, then pop the locating pins down again. Your board will be perfectly positioned time after time.

Heavy Duty Steel Construction

The Tekcel Verso features a heavy duty steel frame and gantry. The frame and gantry are precision machined and welded to ensure outstanding rigidity. This will remove any potential for vibration, which could otherwise affect cut quality. The construction of the frame also includes forklift pick up points to assist with easy transportation and installation onsite. The router bed is constructed from heavy duty reinforced aluminium extrusions which retain their shape even under stress from the strongest vacuum pressure. When topped with rigid foam and precision milled the tables are so flat they can be used for cut to mask applications (cut the material and leave the protective paper intact).

First Class Aftersales Support

ADS team has inhand experience in selling of CNC routers from 1989 onwards. The Tekcel Verso comes with installation and training included throughout Middle East, Africa, India and Pakistan. Once the on-site training is completed you have access to free phone support and online support if you have broadband internet. This ensures any "how do I?" questions can be quickly answered by our tech support team. We have dedicated service technicians on hand to cover maintenance calls and deal with service issues. We also carry an extensive range of spare parts. At ADS support comes first.

Automatic Tool Length Sensor

The Tekcel Wireless Auto Tool Length Sensor ensures the machine can keep track of the length of the tool you are using. If it knows the length of the tool you will get extremely accurate cutting depths and will not be able to cut into the bed of the table. Even if you accidentally program the machine to cut too deep it will not continue past the top of the bed. Incidentally the Tekcel keeps track of its X and Y positions at all times too. This ensures you can not crash the gantry on any axis, providing safety and eliminating expensive service calls.

User Friendly Controls

The Tekcel Verso features Tekmov - the most user friendly and easiest operating system to use and learn. The Tekmov joystick control is conveniently mounted on the machine ensuring there is no risk of damaging the controller by dropping it. Menu Read Outs allow operators to adjust settings on the fly, at any time during the job. Job numbers are virtually unlimited and can be organised in standard folders like on any computer. Tekmov even allows managers to limit the features on the display to ensure operators can't alter files if you don't want them to. Tekmov will log jobs and times in convenient txt file, and provides convenient diagnostic tools in the unlikely event of technical problems.

The Tekcel Verso comes with ProfileLab 2D which features 2D shape and text design plus full 2D toolpath generation. There is no limit to the number of toolpaths in a job, and male, female, hog, online and drilling paths can be easily created and then saved as templates for future use. ProfileLab 2D can be upgraded at any time to add features like bulk DXF importing, true shape nesting, 3D carve, special effects, advanced text, and vectorising.
